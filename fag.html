Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes.

What services do you offer?

+

We offer a comprehensive range of web development services including responsive website design, front-end development, website maintenance, SEO optimization, and performance tuning. Our team specializes in creating custom solutions tailored to your business needs.

How long does it take to build a website?

+

The timeline depends on the complexity and scope of your project. A basic website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects can take 8-12 weeks or longer. We'll provide a detailed timeline after discussing your requirements.

What is your pricing structure?

+

We offer custom pricing based on project requirements. Factors that affect cost include design complexity, number of pages, functionality needs, and integrations. We provide transparent quotes after our initial consultation.

Do you provide website hosting?

+

Yes, we offer reliable hosting solutions with 99.9% uptime, automatic backups, and security monitoring. We can also help you migrate an existing site to our hosting or assist with setting up hosting elsewhere.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

+

Absolutely! All websites we build are fully responsive and optimized for all devices, from desktop to mobile. We follow mobile-first design principles to ensure the best user experience across all screen sizes.

What happens after my website launches?

+

We provide training on managing your site and offer ongoing maintenance packages. We're always available for updates, enhancements, and technical support to ensure your website continues to perform at its best.
